📚 Contenthash Setup Guide

×

Follow these steps to configure a contenthash record for your ENS domain:

1Prepare Your Content

First, you need to upload your website or application to a decentralized storage network like IPFS.

2Get Your Content Hash

Once uploaded, you'll receive a content identifier (CID) that looks like this:

bafy...

3Open ENS Manager

Go to app.ens.domains and connect your wallet that owns the ENS domain.

4Navigate to Your Domain

Search for and select your ENS domain from your account.

5Add Contenthash Record

In the Records section, find "Content Hash" and click "Edit". Enter your content identifier with the appropriate prefix (IPFS example):

ipfs://bafy...

6Confirm Transaction

Save the changes and confirm the transaction in your wallet. The update may take a few minutes to propagate.

7Test Your Domain

Once confirmed, try accessing your ENS domain through a supporting browser or service like eth.limo.

Need more help? Visit the ENS Records documentation for detailed information.
